Guided Support

I can be a little slow or unresponsive with my feelings sometimes. When my brain goes into overdrive or crashes, please use this cheat sheet to know how to support me.

⚠️ Signs that I am in a Manic Episode

What to watch out for (For Friends & Family):

  • Super Hyper or Overdrive: Gising kahit madaling araw, zero sleep pero shockingly puno pa rin ng energy.
  • Racing Thoughts: Pure unfiltered stream of consciousness. I will speak super fast and skip from one random topic to another. Hindi ako natigil magsalita.
  • God Mode Confidence: I will feel like I can conquer the world, make risky plans, or act like I am invincible.
  • Impulsive Splurging: Add to cart and checkout processing endlessly. Big risk for sudden major expenses.
🛡️ Mania Survival Guide: What to Do

How you can protect and help me:

  • Secure My Funds: Please take over my wallet temporarily. Intercept my funds bago ko pa maubos.
  • Lock My Digital Access: Strongly restrict or lock my e-commerce apps (Shopee, Lazada), Google Play Store, and my credit cards. Prevent me from making financial mistakes.
  • Force a Chill Routine: Bring down my energy by tracking my proper sleep schedule and dragging me out for a quiet daily walk to ground my feet.
  • Cozy Movie Nights: Dim the lights, turn on a comfort movie, and keep things quiet. It helps force my brain to pause.
  • Hygiene & Meals Monitor: I might forget to look after myself because of the rush. Kindly remind me to eat on time and do my essential hygiene routine.
