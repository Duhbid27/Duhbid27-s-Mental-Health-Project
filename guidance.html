Guided Support

I can be a little slow with my feelings. When I seem off, these behavior cues and supportive actions help me most.

Feeling overwhelmed

Behavior: I may withdraw, speak quickly, or seem distracted.

What helps: Give me space to breathe, ask if I want help, and remind me it's okay to pause.

Feeling anxious

Behavior: I may fidget, repeat myself, or struggle to focus.

What helps: Listen without pressure, offer a calm distraction, and ask what small step would help most.

Feeling low

Behavior: I may seem quiet, slow to respond, or appear unmotivated.

What helps: Check in gently, show kindness, and encourage rest or a walk without trying to fix everything.

Feeling irritable

Behavior: I may snap, avoid conversation, or seem easily frustrated.

What helps: Respect my need for quiet, avoid arguments, and let me know you'll be there when I’m ready to talk.
