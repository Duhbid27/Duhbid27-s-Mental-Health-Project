Guided Support

I can be a little slow or unresponsive with my feelings sometimes. When my brain goes into overdrive or crashes, please use this cheat sheet to know how to support me.

🚨 SOS Word: Manic Overdrive
àrd

Kapag i-text ko sa inyo 'to out of nowhere, it means my brain is hitting extreme overdrive. Please restrict my e-commerce apps and step in immediately.

🚨 SOS Word: Depressive Crash
ìosal

Kapag sinend ko 'to, it means I've completely crashed and cannot form full words. No need to wait for replies, please just check up on me physically.

⚠️ Signs that I am in a Manic Episode

What to watch out for (For Friends & Family):

  • Malikot & Sobran Daldal: Hindi ako mapakali sa isang lugar, mabilis gumalaw, at mapapansin mong sobrang daldal ko kumpara sa normal—puno ng walang tigil na kuwento.
  • Super Hyper or Overdrive: Gising kahit madaling araw, zero sleep pero shockingly puno pa rin ng energy.
  • Racing Thoughts: Pure unfiltered stream of consciousness. I will speak super fast and skip from one random topic to another.
  • God Mode Confidence: I will feel like I can conquer the world, make risky plans, or act like I am invincible.
  • Impulsive Splurging: Add to cart and checkout processing endlessly. Big risk for sudden major expenses.
🛡️ Mania Survival Guide: What to Do

How you can protect and help me:

  • Secure My Funds: Please take over my wallet temporarily. Intercept my funds bago ko pa maubos.
  • Lock My Digital Access: Strongly restrict or lock my e-commerce apps (Shopee, Lazada), Google Play Store, and my credit cards. Prevent me from making financial mistakes.
  • Force a Chill Routine: Bring down my energy by tracking my proper sleep schedule and dragging me out for a quiet daily walk to ground my feet.
  • Cozy Movie Nights: Dim the lights, turn on a comfort movie, and keep things quiet. It helps force my brain to pause.
  • Hygiene & Meals Monitor: I might forget to look after myself because of the rush. Kindly remind me to eat on time and do my essential hygiene routine.
📉 Signs that I am in a Depressive Episode

What to watch out for (For Friends & Family):

  • Silent Clinginess or Ghosting: Kapag magkasama tayo in person, mapapansin mong tahimik lang ako pero sobrang clingy o laging nakadikit sa tabi mo. Kapag naman online, pwedeng maging sobrang clingy ko sa chat, O KAYA naman ay bigla akong matagal mag-reply o tuluyang hindi sumasagot sa kahit anong social media app. Parehas itong senyales na nahihirapan ang utak ko.
  • High-Functioning Facade: I might look completely operational on the outside—masking it by still showing up to work, studying for law school, or ticking off tasks. But deep inside, it's pure autopilot and hollow exhaustion.
  • High Rejection Sensitivity: My brain plays extreme tricks on me when I'm down. If you don't reply immediately, or if your chat vibe changes (like sending short messages or no emojis), my brain automatically interprets it as rejection or na galit kayo sa akin.
  • Passive Suicidal Ideation: Sometimes, the mental drain triggers quiet, dark coping thoughts. I might drop passive hints like wishing I just wouldn't wake up tomorrow, feeling like everything would be easier if I simply disappeared. It's an expression of intense exhaustion, not a specific active plan.
  • Total Social Withdrawal: Complete silence. I will lock myself in my room, avoid conversations, or ghost group chats.
  • Extreme Fatigue: Finding it super hard to get out of bed. Mabigat ang buong katawan ko, parang laging naka-stop ang mundo.
  • Zero Motivation: Losing interest even in things that usually bring me joy or comfort. Empty and hopeless vibe.
  • Neglecting Self-Care: Skipping meals or just completely losing the energy to look after my physical space.
🌱 Depression Comfort Guide: What to Do

How you can support and pull me through:

  • Create a Safe Space & Boost My Happy Hormones: Kapag mababa ang emosyon ko, kailangan ko ng matinding comfort at safe space na walang judgments. Tulungan mo akong i-boost ang aking happy hormones sa pamamagitan ng pagpapadala ng maiinit na messages, o simpleng pagsama sa akin para hindi ko maramdamang mag-isa ako.
  • Physical Touch & Grounding: Kailangan ko ng tactile connection para humupa ang anxiety ko. Give me tight hugs (yakap), o kaya ay maupo lang nang magkatabi at magkadikit ang balat natin para maramdaman kong ligtas ako. Hikayatin mo rin akong lumabas para masikatan ng araw (sunlight) para mag-ground ang katawan ko.
  • Validate, Don't Fix: Make sure I know that I am super loved, safe, and deeply important to you. You don't need to fix my mood, just sit with me through the heavy weight.
  • Acknowledge and Secure, Don't Panic: If I mention wanting to disappear or sleep forever, please do not freak out, lecture me, or dismiss it as "drama." Listen with calm empathy. Validate how tired I must feel, and remind me gently of my importance.
  • Over-communicate Tone Safety: Please extend a bit more patience with your text style. To protect me from overthinking, use extra warmth, add emojis, or explicitly reassure me by saying, *“Hey, busy lang ako but we are totally good ha!”*
  • Gentle Daily Walks: Don't let me stay cooped up. Kindly encourage me to step outside for a light daily walk. Getting moving helps shift the stagnation.
  • Low-Pressure Movie Nights: Propose a movie night together. No intense talking or deep life questioning required—just purely staying by my side in silence.
  • Basic Hygiene & Nutrition Guard: When the low hits, taking care of my body is hard. Please check if I’ve eaten properly and gently guide me to do my basic daily hygiene.
