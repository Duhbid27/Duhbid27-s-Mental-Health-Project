Digital Letters

Express your thoughts and feelings through different letter formats designed for various levels of sharing and connection.

Open Letters

Share letters on a public board for anyone to read. No specific receiver needed - perfect for general encouragement, reflections, or community support.

Name-Addressed Letters

Write letters addressed to specific names or search for letters written for you. A personal way to connect with others who share your name.

Private Greeting Cards

Create personalized digital greeting cards for special occasions. Send unique links to specific recipients - only they can access and read your message.

Made with care, honesty, awareness, and a little JavaScript.
